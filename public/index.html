About NAK

NAK Squad has been running gaming servers for several years now. Although we have expanded into other games, our roots are in Arma. Our community has over 16 years of experience within the Battlefield franchise. Originally formed to play Battlefield 1942, the community has grown to become one of the largest in the world. NAK currently has over 100,000 registered members with an average of 500 active players per day.
